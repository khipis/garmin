Gobblet Mini — Stack, Cover, Win

A compact wrist adaptation of the Gobblet stacking game. Place your pieces on a 4×4 grid — but a larger piece can gobble up any smaller one. Only the top piece counts, so every move can flip the board in your favour.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — challenge a strategic computer that plans traps ahead
• Player vs Player — compete against a friend on the same device
• AI vs AI — watch the AIs stack and counter each other

SIDE SELECTION
• Choose to play Light (goes first) or Dark (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI plays loosely, giving you room to develop strategy
• Normal — the AI wins when it can and blocks your visible threats
• Hard — look-ahead AI that plans traps and punishes exposed stacks

HOW IT WORKS
• Each player has pieces in three sizes: small, medium, large
• A larger piece can be placed on top of any smaller piece — hiding it
• Only the top-visible piece on each square counts for the win condition
• First to show four of their pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical or diagonal) wins

FEATURES
• Compact 4×4 grid — fast games with real tactical depth
• Visual size-picker when selecting pieces from your hand
• Stacked pieces rendered with layered display
• Session win tracker across multiple rounds
• Zero-allocation gameplay engine

Works on fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach watches.
