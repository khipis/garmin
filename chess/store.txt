Bitochi Chess — Full Chess on Your Wrist

A complete, fully legal chess engine built for Garmin smartwatches. From castling and en passant to pawn promotion — every rule is implemented. Play anywhere, no phone required.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a genuine chess engine with material, positional and king-safety evaluation
• Player vs Player — pass-and-play on one device; board orientation flips between turns so both players see their pieces from the correct side
• AI vs AI — watch two computer opponents battle for supremacy

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — relaxed play with random variation, ideal for beginners
• Normal — solid positional AI, a fair challenge for casual players
• Hard — deep evaluation with material balance, king safety, pawn structure and piece activity bonuses

FEATURES
• Full legal move generation — castling (king-side and queen-side), en passant, pawn promotion
• Piece picker overlay for pawn promotion (choose Queen, Rook, Bishop or Knight)
• Checkmate and stalemate detection
• King-in-check highlight (red square flashes danger)
• Cursor cycles through your pieces then their legal targets — no mis-taps
• Board flips for Black to move in Player vs Player mode — both sides play from their own perspective
• Clean piece rendering readable even on small watch faces
• D-pad or tap controls — fully playable without touchscreen

Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu, MARQ and Approach models.
