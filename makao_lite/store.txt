Makao Lite — Card Game for Your Wrist

`Makao (the popular Central European card game similar to Crazy Eights) comes to your Garmin watch. Match cards by rank or suit, unleash special cards and empty your hand before the AI does.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — play against a strategic computer opponent
• Player vs Player — both players take turns on the same watch
• AI vs AI — watch two AIs play out a full hand

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI plays more randomly, giving you a comfortable lead
• Normal — the AI prefers special cards and tries to empty its hand fast
• Hard — maximises special card impact and chains draw penalties aggressively

SPECIAL CARDS (5 types)
• 2 / 3 — force the opponent to draw 2 or 3 cards (stackable — 2+2=4, 2+3=5...)
• Jack — demand any rank; opponent must play that rank or draw
• Ace — change the active suit for the next play
• King of Hearts — opponent must draw 5 cards

FEATURES
• Full 52-card deck with proper shuffle
• Graphical card faces with suit symbols — clear on any screen size
• All hand cards visible in one row — dynamic sizing fits any hand size
• Selected card lifts and enlarges for easy identification when holding many cards
• Card description label below hand (rank + suit + effect hint)
• AI hand fan shows opponent's card count at a glance
• Active suit displayed beside the discard pile after an Ace is played
• Wrap-around card navigation — scroll past the last card to jump back to the first
• Pending draw counter shown prominently during chain attacks
• Live session win counter for both sides
• Zero-allocation game loop for smooth wrist-side play

Works on fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach watches.
