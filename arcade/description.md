# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Arcade Shooter – Space Invaders Watch

Classic space shooter brought to your Garmin watch. Defend against wave after wave of descending enemies.

### Features
- Waves of enemies with increasing difficulty
- Player cannon at the bottom — dodge and shoot
- Score tracking across waves
- Retro pixel-style rendering optimised for watch screens
- Tap or button controls

### Controls
- **UP / DOWN** — move left/right
- **SELECT** — fire
- **BACK** — exit

### Compatibility
Fenix 5–8, Epix 2, Forerunner series, Instinct, Venu, Vivoactive and more.
