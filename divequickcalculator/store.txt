Dive Quick Calculator — Instant Gas Safety Check

Opens directly on a working calculator — zero navigation, zero delay. The fastest way to check whether your gas is safe for your planned dive, right from your watch.

NOT a dive computer. NOT a decompression planner. An emergency-ready quick reference tool.

TWO INSTANT CALCULATORS

Gas Check (Page 1)
Select your gas mix and depth. Instantly see:
• PO2 value with colour-coded SAFE / WARNING / DANGER status
• Maximum Operating Depth for your gas
• NDL limit at your depth (PADI RDP simplified values)
• Background tints green, orange or red — readable at a glance

Best Mix (Page 2)
Enter your target depth. Instantly see the optimal nitrox fraction that maximises NDL while keeping PO2 within safe limits, plus the resulting MOD.
