Classic Blackjack on your wrist. Beat the dealer to 21 — no casino required.

**How to play:**
- Up / Select = HIT (draw a card)
- Down / Menu = STAND (hold your hand)
- Tap HIT or STAND buttons on screen

**Rules:**
- Standard blackjack: get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over
- Dealer hits until 17 or higher
- Aces count as 1 or 11 automatically
- Start with 500 chips, bet 50 per round

**Features:**
- Clean readable cards on black background
- Instant dealing animation
- Chips tracking across hands
- Auto-reshuffles when deck runs low

Fast, clean, and classic — the only card game you need on your Garmin.
