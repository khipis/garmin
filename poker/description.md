# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Video Poker – Casino Watch Game

Classic Jacks-or-Better video poker on your wrist. Hold your cards, draw replacements, and aim for the best hand.

### Features
- Standard Jacks-or-Better rules
- Hold / draw mechanics
- Standard poker hand payouts (Royal Flush → Jacks or Better)
- Credit tracking across rounds
- Large card display — readable without squinting

### Controls
- **UP / DOWN** — move cursor between cards
- **SELECT** — hold / unhold selected card
- **Long press** — deal next hand

### Compatibility
Fenix 5–8, Epix 2, Forerunner series, Instinct, Venu, Vivoactive and more.
