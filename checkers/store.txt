Bitochi Checkers — Classic Board Game

Play the timeless game of Checkers right on your wrist. A full, polished draughts experience optimised for Garmin watches — mandatory captures, king promotion and a smart AI. No phone needed.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — challenge a look-ahead AI that forces captures and crowns kings
• Player vs Player — pass-and-play on a single watch with a friend
• AI vs AI — sit back and watch two AIs battle it out

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — a forgiving opponent, great for beginners
• Normal — balanced challenge for casual players
• Hard — aggressive look-ahead AI that punishes mistakes and plans forced capture sequences

FEATURES
• Full 8×8 board with mandatory captures and king promotion
• Multi-jump chains supported — if a further capture is available, it must be taken
• Highlight system shows valid moves at a glance
• Board orientation stays consistent for both players in PvP mode
• Session score tracker across multiple games
• D-pad controls — works on all supported watches
• Zero loading screens — starts instantly

Perfect for quick sessions during a rest day, travel, or any spare moment. Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
