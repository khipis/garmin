Hex Mini — Connect Across the Board

A clean implementation of the classic Hex connection game on a compact 7×7 board. Place stones, build a connected path from your edge to the opposite side, and stop your opponent from doing the same.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a computer with centre preference and path-flooding strategy
• Player vs Player — take turns on one watch with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch two AIs fight for the crossing

SIDE SELECTION
• Choose to play Red (connects top–bottom, goes first) or Blue (connects left–right, goes second)

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI makes occasional suboptimal moves
• Normal — solid positional play with centre bias
• Hard — stronger path evaluation that pressures your connection aggressively

HOW IT WORKS
• Red connects the top edge to the bottom; Blue connects left to right
• Each hex cell can be occupied by only one player — no captures
• The game ends instantly when one player completes an unbroken chain
• Win detected using flood-fill (BFS) across the board

FEATURES
• 7×7 hexagonal grid rendered cleanly on round Garmin watches
• D-pad cursor: one button cycles right along the row (wraps), the other cycles down the column (wraps)
• Visual path highlight when the game ends
• Zero-allocation engine — smooth performance on all devices

A timeless abstract strategy game in your pocket. Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
