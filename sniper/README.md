# BitochiSniper

Sniper hunting game for Garmin smartwatches.

## How to Play

1. Scan the terrain through your scope
2. Aim with wrist tilt (accelerometer)
3. Tap to shoot when a creature is in your crosshair
4. Hit the kill target before time runs out

## Features

- Accelerometer-based scope aiming
- Parallax terrain with swaying grass
- Realistic scope overlay with lens rings
- Multiple creature types with unique sprites
- Limited ammo per level
- Time-limited rounds with kill targets
- Hit effects with particle flashes
- Shell casing ejection animation
- Vibration feedback on shots
- Score tracking with best score

## Controls

- **Tilt** — aim scope
- **Tap** — shoot / advance

## Progression

- Increasing kill targets per level
- Faster and more elusive creatures
- Ammo management becomes critical
