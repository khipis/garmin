## Angry Pomodoro – ADHD Focus Timer

A Pomodoro timer that **fights back** when you check your watch.

Designed specifically for ADHD brains that can't stop looking at the watch during focus sessions — the app uses behavioral interruption instead of passive counting.

### How it works

Start a 10, 25, or 45 minute focus session. The watch screen goes calm and minimal (breathing ring + countdown). **Every time you raise your wrist or open the app during focus**, it triggers the interruption overlay:

- Full-screen red flash
- Angry tomato face staring at you
- "GO BACK TO TASK!" message
- Screen shake + vibration pulse
- Timer still visible — because the session keeps running

This isn't punishment. It's a **pattern interrupt** — the same tool used in ADHD coaching to break the distraction loop before it spirals.

### Modes

- **IDLE** — calm neutral face, minimal dark UI
- **FOCUS** — breathing ring animation, large countdown, calm blue tones
- **INTERRUPT** — rage overlay triggered by wrist raise during focus
- **END** — comical rage animation, "GREAT FOCUS!" reward, strong vibration

### Timer presets
10 / 25 / 45 minutes

### ADHD design note

Most timers reward you for ignoring them. This one makes ignoring it feel intentional — you see the timer, acknowledge it, and actively choose to return to work. That moment of conscious redirection is the whole point.

_Not a medical tool. Just a watch app with opinions._
