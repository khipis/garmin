# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Fish – Relaxing Aquarium Watch Game

A calming virtual aquarium on your watch. Feed fish, watch them grow, keep your tank thriving.

### Features
- Animated fish swimming across the watch face
- Feed your fish and keep them happy
- Fish grow over time with good care
- Minimal interaction — check in when you want
- Soothing, low-key design

### Controls
- **SELECT** — feed fish
- **BACK** — exit

### Compatibility
Fenix 5–8, Epix 2, Forerunner series, Instinct, Venu, Vivoactive and more.
