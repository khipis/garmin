Sensor integration

Your body, measured in real time

Heart Rate Tracking
Monitors HR baseline, peak and drop throughout every session. Tracks the physiological response to breathwork over time.
Body Battery Monitoring
Snapshots body battery at session start and end so you can see the real recovery effect of each breathing block.
Calm Score
Rates the quality of each breathwork session 0–100. Builds an exponential moving average across all sessions for trend tracking.
Activity Recording
Every session logs a Garmin activity — visible in Garmin Connect with full sensor timeline.
Apnea PB Tracking
Personal best breath-hold time recorded per session with a delta shown at every attempt — always know if you beat your record.
Weekly Consistency
7-day and 14-day session streaks visualised on screen. Build the habit and track it.
Haptic Cues
Vibration alerts guide you through each breath phase — no need to look at the screen during holds.
Pre-start Alert
Configurable vibration warning (3s / 5s / 10s / 15s) before each breath hold begins.
