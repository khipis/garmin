# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Pets – Virtual Pet Watch Companion

Your personal Tamagotchi on your Garmin watch. Feed, play with, and care for your virtual pet.

### Features
- Animated pet with hunger, happiness, and energy stats
- Feed and play interactions
- Pet reacts to neglect — keep it happy
- Persistent state — your pet lives on even when the app is closed
- Simple, charming pixel-style graphics

### Controls
- **UP / DOWN** — choose action (feed, play, rest)
- **SELECT** — confirm action
- **BACK** — exit

### Compatibility
Fenix 5–8, Epix 2, Forerunner series, Instinct, Venu, Vivoactive and more.
