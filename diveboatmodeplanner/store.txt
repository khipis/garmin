Dive Boat Mode Planner — On-Deck Go/No-Go Tool

A fast, outdoor-optimised dive planning tool designed for use on the boat between dives. Maximum legibility in bright sunlight — large coloured verdict boxes readable at arm's length.

NOT a dive computer. NOT a decompression planner. A pre-dive decision tool.

THREE PLANNING MODES

Quick Plan
Enter your gas mix, target depth, planned time, tank fill pressure and tank size. Get an instant GO / REVIEW / NO-GO verdict based on PO2 safety, NDL limit and gas duration. One screen, one answer.

Safety Check
Enter gas mix and depth. See your PO2 value in large coloured digits with a SAFE / WARNING / DANGER classification. Instantly know whether your gas is safe for your planned depth.

Gas Duration
Enter tank size, fill pressure, SAC rate and planned depth. Get remaining bottom time in minutes — know exactly when you need to turn around.

FEATURES
• Near-black background with maximum-brightness verdict boxes
• Colour-coded status: green (safe), amber (review), red (no-go)
• No confirmation steps — result updates live as you scroll inputs
• Works without phone connectivity — fully offline
• Designed for gloves-on, sunglasses-on, on-deck use

Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
