## Boat Mode Dive Planner – MOD NDL Gas Check

Built for one place: the boat deck, in full sun, wetsuit on, 60 seconds before the dive.

Three modes. Large numbers. Clear verdict. No menus.

---

### Three Modes

**QUICK PLAN**
Enter gas, depth, planned time, and tank fill.
Get a single GO / REVIEW / NO GO verdict — instantly.
All three safety checks (PO2, NDL, gas) are evaluated in parallel and the worst result drives the verdict.

**SAFETY CHECK**
Enter gas and depth only.
Get a large PO2 reading (colored: green / amber / red) plus SAFE / WARNING / DANGER verdict.
MOD and NDL shown below for full context.

**GAS CHECK**
Enter fill pressure, tank size, depth, and planned time.
See how many minutes your gas will last at a standard 18 L/min SAC.
ENOUGH or SHORT — and by how many minutes.

---

### Why Outdoor-Optimised?

- Near-black background, maximum-brightness signal colors
- Verdict boxes fill the lower third of the screen in bright green, amber, or red
- All key numbers rendered in the largest available font
- No tiny text, no sub-menus, no loading

---

### Controls

- **UP / DOWN** — cycle preset values (gas, depth, time, fill, tank)
- **SELECT** — advance to next input field
- **BACK** — step back / return to mode select
- **Long-press MENU** — jump back to mode select from anywhere
- **Swipe left/right** — next field / back
- **Tap upper / lower half** — adjust value up or down

---

### Calculations

| Output       | Formula                                               |
|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| PO2          | FO2 × (depth ÷ 10 + 1)                               |
| MOD          | (1.4 ÷ FO2 − 1) × 10                                 |
| NDL          | PADI recreational table + EAD correction for nitrox   |
| Gas duration | (fill − 50 bar) × tank ÷ (SAC × ambient pressure)    |

Gas assumptions: SAC = 18 L/min, reserve = 50 bar.
