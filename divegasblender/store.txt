Dive Gas Blender — Nitrox PP Blending Calculator

A precise partial-pressure nitrox blending tool for divers who blend their own gas. Calculate exact fill pressures, verify your achieved mix and look up MOD values — all from your wrist.

THREE TOOLS IN ONE

PP Blend (Partial Pressure Blending)
Select your target nitrox mix (21%–40%), target fill pressure and starting pressure in the tank. Get the exact O2 fill pressure and air top-up pressure to achieve your blend.

Mix Check (Blend Verification)
After blending, enter your O2 fill pressure, air top-up pressure and starting pressure. Instantly verify the actual FO2 achieved — confirm your blend before diving.

MOD Lookup
Select any gas mix and get a full PO2 table showing Maximum Operating Depth at 1.4 bar (working limit) and 1.6 bar (absolute limit). Quick reference for any fill.

FEATURES
• Covers Air 21% through Nitrox 40% in fine increments
• All pressures in bar (standard metric blending units)
• Live result updates as you scroll — no confirmation button needed
• Fully offline — no connectivity required
• Clean, readable display optimised for bright outdoor conditions

For recreational nitrox divers and fill station operators. Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
