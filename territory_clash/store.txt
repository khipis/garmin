Territory Clash — Go-Style Strategy Game

Control more territory than your opponent in this streamlined Go-inspired strategy game. Place stones, surround groups to capture them, and claim the most space on a 9×9 board.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a computer with centre bias and capture awareness
• Player vs Player — alternate turns on one watch with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch two AIs carve up the board

COLOUR SELECTION
• Choose to play Black (goes first) or White (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI plays loosely, ideal for learning the mechanics
• Normal — centre preference with basic threat response
• Hard — stronger capture and territory evaluation with look-ahead

HOW IT WORKS
• Black and White alternate placing stones on a 9×9 grid
• A group with no empty adjacent points (liberties) is captured and removed
• Suicide moves are prevented automatically
• The game ends when both players pass (BACK key) or the board fills
• Score = territory controlled + captured stones

FEATURES
• Simplified Go rules — accessible without memorising complex ko or chain logic
• BFS-based group detection and capture
• Star point markers for quick orientation
• D-pad cursor: one button cycles right along the row (wraps), other cycles down the column (wraps)
• Live capture count displayed during play
• BACK key passes your turn; second consecutive pass ends the game
• Zero-allocation engine — smooth on all supported devices

A perfect introduction to Go-style thinking in a fast, accessible format. Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
