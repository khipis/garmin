Dive Plan Toolkit — Gas & Planning Calculators

A comprehensive set of dive planning calculators for recreational scuba divers. Fast, reliable and fully offline — everything you need before and between dives, right on your watch.

NOT a dive computer. NOT a decompression planner. A pre-dive decision-support tool.

FIVE CALCULATORS

MOD / PO2
Is your gas safe at this depth? What is your maximum operating depth? Enter your FO2 and depth — get PO2 and a SAFE / WARNING / DANGER verdict instantly.

Best Mix
What nitrox fraction should you fill for your target depth? Get the optimal FO2, MOD and EAD values to maximise NDL while staying within safe PO2 limits.

EAD (Equivalent Air Depth)
What air depth has the same nitrogen loading as your nitrox at this depth? Use EAD to look up NDL on standard air tables.

Gas Usage / SAC Rate
What is your surface air consumption rate from a completed dive? Enter start/end pressure, tank volume, depth and time — get your SAC in bar/min.

NDL Limit
How long can you stay at your planned depth without deco? Based on simplified PADI RDP values for air dives.
