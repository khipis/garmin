# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Dive Gas & Planning Toolkit – MOD NDL

Professional dive planning reference tool for recreational scuba divers. Five calculators in one app — all in large, readable numbers.

**This app is NOT a dive computer. For planning and reference only.**

---

### Five Calculators

**MOD / PO2 Calculator**
Enter gas (Air, Nitrox 32/36) and depth. Get PO2 immediately with a GREEN (safe) or RED (exceeds limit) indicator. MOD shown below.

**Best Mix Calculator**
Enter planned depth — get the optimal nitrox mix for PO2 = 1.4.

**EAD (Equivalent Air Depth)**
Compares nitrogen load of your nitrox dive to an equivalent air dive. Useful for NDL cross-reference.

**Gas Consumption (SAC)**
Enter tank size, start/end pressure, dive time, and depth. Get your SAC rate in L/min.

**NDL Limit**
PADI-based no-decompression limit table with interpolation and EAD correction for nitrox.

---

### Design

- Extreme readability: all key numbers in the largest available font
- Full-screen layouts with safety color coding
- Maximum 2–3 button presses to a result
- Gas presets: Air 21%, Nitrox 32, Nitrox 36
