# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Parachute – Skydiving Watch Game

Guide your skydiver through the sky, deploy the parachute at the right moment, and land in the target zone.

### Features
- Freefall phase: steer left and right to avoid obstacles
- Timed parachute deploy — too early or too late affects your score
- Landing zone targeting — precision matters
- Wind drift factor adds challenge
- Score based on landing accuracy

### Controls
- **UP / DOWN** — steer during freefall
- **SELECT** — deploy parachute
- **BACK** — exit

### Compatibility
Fenix 5–8, Epix 2, Forerunner series, Instinct, Venu, Vivoactive and more.
