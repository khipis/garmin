# Garmin Connect IQ Store Description

## Mini Golf – Putt Watch Puzzle Game

Miniature golf on your wrist. Aim, choose your power, and putt the ball through 20 hand-crafted holes.

### Features
- **20 unique holes** — straight intros, doglegs, Z-chicane, island green, animated windmill, diamond court, bowtie/hourglass, funnel, slalom, the eye, water-tunnel, pinball arena, cross fairway, volcano with water moat, swinging pendulums, triangle, spiral and a boss finale
- **Two visual themes** — classic white rails and chunky brown bumper rails (springier rebound) for that pinball feel
- **Realistic physics** — natural rolling friction, elastic bounce, lip-out near the cup, water hazard penalty
- Angle and power control with adaptive sub-stepping
- Stroke count tracking with two-column scorecard and Eagle / Birdie / Par / Bogey ratings
- Clean, top-down view that scales to any round or rectangular display

### Controls
- **UP / DOWN** — adjust aim direction
- **SELECT** — set power / putt
- **BACK** — exit / hole menu

### Compatibility
Fenix 5–8, Epix 2, Forerunner series, Instinct, Venu, Vivoactive and more.
