# Diver Communicator

Communicate underwater without hand signals — even in complete darkness or zero visibility.

Show large, high-contrast messages on your Garmin watch. Point your wrist at your dive buddy and let the screen do the talking.

**5 Message Categories (27 messages total):**

🟢 STATUS — OK, ALL GOOD, WAIT, GOOD TO GO, CHECK ME
🔵 AIR — AIR OK?, HOW MUCH AIR?, LOW AIR!, SHARE AIR?, CHECK AIR
🟡 MOVEMENT — GO UP, ASCEND NOW, FOLLOW ME, STOP, TURN BACK, CHECK DEPTH
⚪ REPLY — YES, NO, UNDERSTOOD, NOT SURE, SAY AGAIN?
🔴 EMERGENCY — HELP!, OUT OF AIR!, ABORT DIVE, COME NOW!, EQUIPMENT!, LOST!

**Design principles:**
- Pure black background for maximum contrast
- Each category uses a unique high-visibility colour (green / cyan / yellow / white / red)
- Messages fill the screen in the largest possible font
- Thick colour border identifies the message category at a glance
- Emergency screen flashes red — impossible to miss

**Controls:**
- Up/Down buttons or swipe to scroll
- Select / tap to show message full-screen
- Hold Menu button → instant HELP! from anywhere
- Navigate between messages without leaving the display screen

**No settings, no accounts, no network required.**
Works offline, underwater, in gloves.

Designed for recreational scuba divers, technical divers, and underwater photographers.

*Not a substitute for proper dive training or a dive computer.*
