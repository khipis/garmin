Othello Blitz — Flip Your Way to Victory

The full Othello (Reversi) experience on your Garmin watch. Outmanoeuvre your opponent, flip their discs and control the board — a timeless strategy game that rewards thinking ahead.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a position-weight AI that values corners and edges
• Player vs Player — take turns on one device with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch two computers flip each other into submission

COLOUR SELECTION
• Choose to play Black (goes first) or White (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI plays with random variation, letting you practice freely
• Normal — strategic play using positional weights (corners are gold)
• Hard — maximises corner and edge control with stronger board evaluation

FEATURES
• Full 8×8 Othello board
• Legal move enforcement — only valid placements allowed
• Disc flip highlights show captured pieces
• Live disc count for both players
• Automatic pass when no legal moves are available
• Score shown throughout the game
• D-pad cursor with row and column wrapping navigation
• Clean disc rendering optimised for round watches

Whether you call it Othello or Reversi, this is the real deal — complete rules, smart AI, instant play. Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
