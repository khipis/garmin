# New Bitochi Games — Connect IQ Store Listings

Store-ready metadata for the brand-new games. Each entry has a catchy title
(under 50 characters) and an English long description (no emoji — the store
does not allow them). All of them are premium idle/progression titles built on
the shared Bitochi menu, save system and global leaderboard.

---

## 1. Bitochi Creatures  (folder: `creatures`)

**Store title (37 chars):**
`Bitochi Creatures: Idle Pet Evolution`

**Description:**

Hatch a mysterious egg and raise a creature that is truly your own. Bitochi
Creatures is an idle evolution game where your pet keeps growing, feeding and
mutating even while you are away — then rewards you the moment you return.

Every creature is procedurally generated from its own DNA: species, traits,
colors and rarity are unique to you, so no two players ever raise the same
companion. Feed it, train it and watch it evolve down different paths depending
on how you play and how you move in real life.

What you can do:
- Hatch an egg and reveal a one-of-a-kind creature with its own DNA and traits.
- Feed, train and explore to earn XP, food and rare DNA mutations.
- Evolve along multiple branches — Runner, Warrior, Dream and Energy forms and
  beyond — shaped by your daily activity (steps, heart rate, sleep).
- Collect offline progress every time you come back, up to 24 hours.
- Complete daily challenges and keep a login streak alive for milestone rewards.
- Track your journey in the creature journal and discover the global creature
  index to see how rare your companion really is.
- Climb global leaderboards: rarest creature, longest-living, highest evolution
  and most active trainer.

Sessions take seconds, but the pull to come back tomorrow lasts for weeks. Your
creature never stops growing.

## 2. Space Colony  (folder: `spacecolony`)

**Store title (33 chars):**
`Space Colony: Idle Sci-Fi Builder`

**Description:**

You are the commander of humanity's first colony on an unknown planet. Space
Colony is an idle civilization builder where your base produces resources,
expands and researches new technology around the clock — even while your watch
is on your wrist and you are living your day.

Return to collect what your colony mined and generated, then invest it: raise
habitats, reactors, mines, farms and laboratories, unlock advanced structures
like launch pads and planetary defense, and push your civilization to the stars.

What you can do:
- Manage five resources — energy, minerals, water, science and credits — that
  accumulate offline for up to 24 hours.
- Build and upgrade a deep structure tree, each level boosting production and
  changing how your colony looks.
- Explore planet regions to discover new resources, blueprints and rare finds.
- React to random events: meteor showers, alien signals, solar storms and lost
  expeditions.
- Complete daily missions and use your real activity to fuel expeditions and
  bonuses.
- Keep a daily streak and hit milestones from first colony to galactic empire.
- Compete on global leaderboards: largest colony, most advanced technology and
  oldest surviving colony.

A polished, addictive "one more upgrade" loop that grows your galaxy while you
sleep.

## 4. Bitochi Mines  (folder: `mines`)

**Store title (34 chars):**
`Bitochi Mines: Idle Digging Tycoon`

**Description:**

Start with a rusty pickaxe and a tiny tunnel, then dig your way to an
underground empire. Bitochi Mines is an idle mining and discovery game where
your miners keep digging deeper and hauling resources while you are away — so
you always come back to something new.

The deeper you go, the stranger it gets: new materials, ancient artifacts, alien
crystals and legendary finds wait in the dark. Upgrade your tools, build your
base and chase the endless question — what is below?

What you can do:
- Bank offline mining progress — stone, iron, gold, gems and depth —md up to 24
  hours.
- Descend through distinct depth zones, from the surface mine to the abyss, each
  with its own materials, events and discoveries.
- Upgrade equipment (pickaxes, carts, drills) and build a base: mine shafts,
  forge, elevator, laboratory, gem workshop and deep scanner.
- Uncover discoveries and fill a rarity-ranked collection, from common ore to
  mythic legendary finds.
- Trigger random events: earthquakes, hidden caves, rich veins and ancient
  machines.
- Complete daily challenges and let your real daily activity power mining
  bonuses.
- Track milestones and a login streak, and climb global leaderboards: deepest
  mine, richest miner, most legendary finds and oldest mine.

A flagship idle title with a satisfying progression loop and constant discovery.

## 5. Farm Life  (folder: `farm`)

**Store title (31 chars):**
`Farm Life: Idle Ranch Builder`

**Description:**

Start with a bare paddock and one lonely hen, then grow it into a bustling,
storybook ranch that keeps producing while you are away. Farm Life is a cozy
idle farm builder where your animals multiply, your crops ripen and your guests
keep arriving — so you always come back to a farm that has grown a little more.

The whole farm lives on your watch face as a hand-crafted pixel diorama: a bright
day-to-night sky with a warm sun, drifting clouds and stars, rolling green
pastures, a rustic fence, and every barn, field and animal you add drawn in
chunky, cheerful pixel art. Raise chickens, ducks, pigs and cows, plant wheat,
carrots, orchards and berry bushes, and watch your little world fill up.

What you can do:
- Bank offline production — coins, wood, grain and feed — up to 24 hours.
- Raise livestock (chicken coop, duck pond, pig pen, cow barn) whose herds grow
  over time, and watch cute animals wander your field.
- Grow crops and orchards, and build a market: farm stand, windmill, bakery and
  petting zoo that draw paying guests.
- Explore the surrounding land — meadow, woods, pond and hills — to unlock
  special landmarks: a Golden Barn, glass Greenhouse, Prize Bull and Rainbow Silo.
- Fill a charm collection of nine decorations, from a flower bed and scarecrow to
  a golden egg, rainbow cow and prize ribbon.
- Trigger random events: storms, lucky crates, stray animals, harvest festivals
  and traveling merchants.
- Complete daily challenges and let your real daily activity power the farm —
  steps explore the land, active minutes boost scouting, sleep grants a night
  harvest.
- Keep a login streak, track milestones, and climb global leaderboards: highest
  level, most charming farm, biggest herd and rarest collection.

Sessions take seconds, but your farm is always growing — beautiful, relaxing and
impossible to put down.
