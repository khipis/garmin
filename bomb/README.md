# BitochiBomb

Retro bomb dropping arcade game for Garmin smartwatches.

## How to Play

1. Steer your plane left/right by tilting your wrist
2. Tap to drop a bomb
3. Bombs fall with gravity and wind drift
4. Hit enemies on the ground for chain reaction explosions

## Features

- Accelerometer-based plane steering
- Gravity and wind physics for bombs
- 8 enemy types: Grunt, Brute, Runner, Tank, Spider, Ogre, Ghost, Swarm
- Chain reaction explosion system
- Particle effects: fire, smoke, blood, water fountains
- Persistent craters on the ground
- Ground decorations: ruined buildings, lamp posts, houses
- Screen shake and vibration scaling with impact
- "Perfect drop" bonus for center hits

## Controls

- **Tilt** — steer plane horizontally
- **Tap** — drop bomb

## Progression

- Waves of increasingly tough enemies
- Wind direction and strength vary
- Score-based progression
