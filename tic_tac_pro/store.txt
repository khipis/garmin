Tic Tac Pro — Scalable N-in-a-Row Strategy

Classic Tic-Tac-Toe expanded into a full strategy game. Choose your grid size from 3×3 to 7×7, pick your side and take on a smart AI — or play against a friend — right on your wrist.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a strategic computer with fork detection and heuristic evaluation
• Player vs Player — take turns on one watch with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch two AIs battle across the board

SIDE SELECTION
• Choose to play X (goes first) or O (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

GRID SIZE
• Select any grid from 3×3 up to 7×7
• Win length adapts to the grid size — larger grids need 4 or 5 in a row

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — random moves with occasional smart plays
• Normal — blocks your threats and builds its own lines
• Hard — fork detection and scored move evaluation; the AI creates double threats you can't block

FEATURES
• Smooth D-pad cursor: left button cycles right along the current row (wraps), right button cycles down the column (wraps)
• Win highlight shows the exact winning line
• Draw detection when the board fills
• Session score tracked across rounds
• Multi-tick AI engine — no slowdowns or watchdog errors even on 7×7

Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
