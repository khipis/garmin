Mini Go 9×9 — Ancient Strategy, Modern Wrist

Play the world's oldest board game on your Garmin watch. A complete 9×9 Go implementation with territory scoring, stone capture, ko rule and a strategic AI — no phone needed.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a computer that values centre control and responds to local threats
• Player vs Player — alternate turns on one device with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch two computer players fight for territory

COLOUR SELECTION
• Choose to play Black (goes first) or White (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI plays loosely with random variation
• Normal — solid positional play, captures when possible
• Hard — stronger territory and capture evaluation with board-wide awareness

FEATURES
• Full 9×9 board — ideal for quick games without sacrificing strategy
• Legal move enforcement including ko rule
• Suicide move prevention
• BFS-based group liberty detection and capture
• Territory + captured stones scoring at game end
• Pass button (BACK key) — game ends when both players pass consecutively
• Star point markers for visual orientation
• D-pad cursor: one button cycles along the row (wraps), other cycles down the column (wraps)
• Clean stone rendering optimised for round watch displays

Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
