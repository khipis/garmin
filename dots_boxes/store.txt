Dots and Boxes — Classic Pencil Game

The beloved pencil-and-paper classic on your Garmin watch. Draw lines between dots, complete squares to score, and outsmart your opponent in this game of cunning and chains.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — face a clever opponent that avoids giving you free boxes
• Player vs Player — take turns on one watch with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch the computer battle itself

SIDE SELECTION
• Choose to play Blue (goes first) or Red (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI makes occasional mistakes, good for learning strategy
• Normal — solid play: grabs boxes when possible and avoids giving chains
• Hard — optimised chain-aware AI that sacrifices short chains to win long ones

FEATURES
• 5×5 dot grid — 40 edges, 16 boxes to claim
• D-pad navigation: one button cycles horizontally (wraps), the other cycles vertically (wraps)
• Your completed boxes shown in blue, opponent's in red
• Score displayed live throughout the game
• Zero-allocation game loop — smooth on all supported devices

A perfect quick-play game for commutes, breaks and waiting rooms. Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
