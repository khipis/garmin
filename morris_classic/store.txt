Morris Classic — Nine Men's Morris on Your Wrist

One of the oldest board games in history, now on your Garmin watch. Place pieces, form mills, remove opponent stones and manoeuvre your way to victory in this deep three-phase strategy game.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — challenge a greedy mill-hunting AI
• Player vs Player — take turns on one watch with a friend
• AI vs AI — watch two AIs grind each other down

SIDE SELECTION
• Choose to play Dark (goes first) or Light (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — the AI plays with noticeable mistakes, great for learning
• Normal — prioritises forming mills and blocking yours
• Hard — stronger evaluation, aggressive mill hunting and defensive blocking

THREE PHASES OF PLAY
• Placing — take turns placing all 9 pieces on the 24-node board
• Moving — slide pieces to adjacent nodes along the board lines
• Flying — when reduced to 3 pieces, move anywhere on the board

MILL MECHANIC
Form a mill (three of your pieces in a line) to remove any opponent piece. Break and re-form mills to keep taking pieces. Reduce your opponent to 2 pieces to win.

FEATURES
• Classic 24-node board rendered clearly on round watches
• Node cursor with D-pad navigation — one button cycles horizontally (wraps), the other vertically (wraps)
• Automatic phase transitions with on-screen hints
• Valid move highlighting
• Compact zero-allocation game engine

Supports fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach models.
