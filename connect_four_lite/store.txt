Connect Four Lite — Drop & Connect

The classic four-in-a-row game perfectly adapted for Garmin watches. Drop discs, build your line, block your opponent — simple to learn, endlessly replayable.

GAME MODES
• Player vs AI — take on a smart computer that plans several moves ahead
• Player vs Player — challenge a friend on the same watch
• AI vs AI — watch two AIs go head to head

SIDE SELECTION
• Choose to play Red (goes first) or Yellow (goes second) in Player vs AI mode

DIFFICULTY
• Easy — random-leaning AI, great for kids and beginners
• Normal — balanced opponent that blocks threats and builds its own
• Hard — aggressive AI that prioritises winning sequences and creates threats

FEATURES
• Classic 7×6 grid with gravity-based disc drops
• Win by connecting 4 discs horizontally, vertically or diagonally
• Win highlight shows the winning four discs
• D-pad cursor: left button cycles columns right (wraps), right button cycles columns left (wraps)
• Session score persists across rounds
• Zero-allocation game loop — fast and smooth on all supported devices

Works on fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach watches.
