Edge Survivor — Circular Action on Your Wrist

You are locked to the edge. Everything wants to kill you. Survive as long as you can in this fast-paced minimalist action game built specifically for round Garmin watches.

HOW TO PLAY
• Your player is pinned to the outer ring of the screen
• Move LEFT and RIGHT along the edge to dodge incoming hazards
• Hazards spawn from the centre and race outward — react fast or die

HAZARDS
• Rotating laser beams — time your movements between sweeps
• Expanding ring walls with a single gap — find the opening and slip through
• Radial bullet spreads — weave between projectiles
• Arc barriers that close off sections of the edge

FEATURES
• Pure reflex gameplay — no menus, no waiting, just survival
• Difficulty ramps automatically as you last longer
• Score based on time survived
• Instant restart — back in action in seconds
• Optimised for round watch displays, pixel-perfect on every supported device

How long can you hold the edge? Works on fenix, epix, Forerunner, vívoactive, venu and Approach watches.
